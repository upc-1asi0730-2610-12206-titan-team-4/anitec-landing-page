Key Features

Everything you need to manage your livestock

A complete platform with tools designed to optimize every aspect of your livestock management.

Health Control

Register and control all vaccinations, treatments and health events of your livestock. Receive automatic alerts to keep your herd healthy and protected.

Reproduction

Control the reproductive cycle of your livestock, register births, mating and weaning. Optimize your herd genetics with genealogy tracking tools.

Economic Management

Control your expenses and earnings. Generate detailed economic reports to make informed decisions about the profitability of your livestock business.

Traceability

Each animal has its complete history. Identify via QR or unique code to maintain full traceability from birth to sale.

Smart Alerts

Receive automatic notifications about upcoming vaccinations, expected births and any important event directly on your phone.

Mobile Access

Access your platform from your phone or tablet anywhere. Works offline where coverage is limited.
